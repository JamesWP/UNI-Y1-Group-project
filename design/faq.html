Frequently Asked Questions

How can I create my own deck of questions?

You need to be logged in to create your own page. Then go to (the create your own) button on the top of the page, choose a subject, and you could generate the questions by typing the question, choosing the type of answers, typing the answers depends on the type. You cannot write less than 10 questions per deck.

What if I forget my password?

There is a forget your password button you could click on it in the login page and that will send your password to your account.

What if I decided to change the subject in the middle of the quiz?

You could always go to the subject button on the top of the page and choosing another subject.

How can I choose a subject?

There would be the list of subjects on the main page, or alternatively you could choose the subject button on the top of the page.

How can I know what decks are there?

You could know the top rated decks from the main page, or you could choose a subject, and all the decks in the subject will be listed.
